African Nebula: UNIOSUN Journal of Humanities was the official peer-reviewed journal of the College of Humanities and Culture at Osun State University (2010–2014).

Apart from its print version which was published locally in Nigeria, African Nebula was also published online as an Australian publication. As a subsidiary publication of Nebula, African Nebula contributed immensely to the promotion and development of global multidisciplinary scholarship generally, and to the dissemination of African intellectual production.

It was one of the first internationally-based, peer reviewed journals dedicated to publishing scholarship from and about Africa.
